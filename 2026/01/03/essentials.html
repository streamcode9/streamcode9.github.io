The Absolute Minimum for Life

Published January 03, 2026

  • life
  • essentials

If we cut to the bone, here's what you cannot ignore:

1. Body & Health — without health everything else is meaningless. Sleep, nutrition, movement, basic first aid.

2. Economics & Finance — personal finance. Not investments, just: income > expenses, emergency fund, don't get into debt.

3. Mind & Psychology — understanding your emotions, habits, thinking traps. Otherwise you sabotage yourself.

4. Relationships & Communication — people = resource, support, meaning. Minimum: ability to listen, say "no", maintain close connections.

5. Work & Career — you need income. That means you need a skill people pay for.

Everything else is superstructure

  • Technology — useful, but you can live without deep knowledge
  • Philosophy — a luxury for those who've covered the basics
  • Art, games, hobbies — recovery, but not survival
  • Science, history, geography — horizons, not necessity

Formula

Health → Money → Mind in order → People nearby → Work

Five pillars. Everything else you add when these are standing.
